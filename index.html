Welcome to Shri Trilok Bharti Public Sr. Sec. School

Shri Trilok Bharti Public Sr. Sec. School is a co-educational English medium school affiliated to the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education, Ajmer. The school is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible from all parts of the city. The school has a well-equipped library, science laboratories, computer lab, and a playground.

About Us

Shri Trilok Bharti Public Sr. Sec. School was established in 2000 by Shri Trilok Bharti Shiksha Samiti. The school is committed to providing quality education to its students. The school has a team of dedicated and experienced teachers who are committed to the overall development of the students.
